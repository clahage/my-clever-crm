How Payment Works

  1. No Upfront Costs: You pay absolutely nothing to get started. No monthly fees, no setup fees.
  2. We Work On Your Case: We submit professional disputes to all three credit bureaus on your behalf.
  3. Confirmed Deletions: When credit bureaus confirm a deletion, we verify it on your updated credit reports.
  4. Invoice Sent: We send you an invoice for successfully deleted items based on the fee schedule above.
  5. Payment Due: Payment is due within 15 days of invoice date.
  6. Zero Risk: If we don't succeed in deleting an item, you owe nothing for that item.

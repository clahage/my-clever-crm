3. Grant of Authority

I, {{CLIENT_NAME}} ("Principal"), hereby grant to Speedy Credit Repair Inc. ("Agent") a LIMITED POWER OF ATTORNEY to act on my behalf for the following specific purposes only:

Authorized Actions:

  • To obtain my credit reports from Equifax, Experian, TransUnion, and any other consumer reporting agencies
  • To review, analyze, and discuss my credit reports with credit bureaus
  • To submit disputes and letters on my behalf to credit bureaus regarding inaccurate, incomplete, unverifiable, or questionable information
  • To communicate with credit bureaus via mail, phone, fax, or electronic means regarding my credit reports
  • To receive responses and updates from credit bureaus regarding disputed items
  • To request investigations and re-investigations of disputed credit report information
  • To request verification and documentation from credit bureaus and creditors
  • To contact creditors, collection agencies, and data furnishers on my behalf regarding items appearing on my credit reports
  • To request goodwill adjustments and payment arrangements with creditors
  • To receive copies of investigation results and updated credit reports

⚠️ IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS:

This Limited Power of Attorney is STRICTLY LIMITED to credit reporting and credit repair purposes as listed above. The Agent does NOT have authority to:

  • Make financial transactions on my behalf
  • Open or close credit accounts
  • Apply for credit in my name
  • Access my bank accounts or financial assets
  • Sign contracts or agreements unrelated to credit repair
  • Make purchases or payments on my behalf (except for credit repair services)
  • Sell, transfer, or mortgage any of my property
  • Make healthcare or personal decisions on my behalf

4. Authorization Under FCRA

Under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), 15 U.S.C. § 1681 et seq., I hereby authorize Speedy Credit Repair Inc. to:

  • Act as my authorized representative in all matters relating to my credit reports
  • Receive my credit reports and credit scores on my behalf
  • Dispute inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable information pursuant to FCRA § 1681i
  • Request reinvestigation of disputed items
  • Communicate with consumer reporting agencies as my representative

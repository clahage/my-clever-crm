6. Service Process and Timeline

Our Standard Process:

  • Initial Analysis: Review of your credit reports to identify inaccurate or questionable items
  • Dispute Strategy: Development of customized dispute strategy for your specific situation
  • Monthly Disputes: Submission of professional disputes to credit bureaus on your behalf
  • Bureau Communication: We handle all communication with credit bureaus (Equifax, Experian, TransUnion)
  • Progress Reports: Monthly updates on dispute results and credit score changes
  • Ongoing Support: Phone and email support throughout your contract period

Estimated Timeline:

Most clients see initial results within 30-60 days, with significant improvements typically occurring over {{CONTRACT_MONTHS}} months. Results vary based on the complexity of your credit issues.
